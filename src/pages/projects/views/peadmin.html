Overview

SPA with SEO-optimized landing page and a modern, interactive web app.

Customers can schedule a pickup and return, choose the items they will be dropping off, add any special instructions, and complete their payment.

Time/Date Manipulation

PROBLEM: The scheduling section requires several different date/time formats for displaying, manipulating, receiving input, transmitting to the server, and storing in the database.
All times must be presented in PST regardless of device clock.

SOLUTOIN: Tried several different libraries including Moment, Dayjs, Luxon, and Date-fns.
Chose Luxon as it was smaller than Moment but also has time zone features. If only user time zone mattered Dayjs would have been the best solution due to size.

Optimization

PROBLEM: The landing page was unable to render in Google Search or Bing Search in order to be indexed.
The page was also loading slow due to render blocking javascript and large images, and scroll events were causing jank.

SOLUTION: Added post-build step to prerender content to static HTML.
Used React.Lazy to load minimum necessary javascript and created useIntersectionObserver hook to lazy load images and add animation without scroll events.
